## Insight 1: Account A3 Shows Early Risk Signals

### Observation
Account A3 has only 1 active day and a single active user, despite using
multiple features.

### Interpretation
This suggests that product usage is concentrated with one individual.
If this user becomes inactive, the account is at high risk of disengagement.
